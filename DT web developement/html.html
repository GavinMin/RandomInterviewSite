What?

One question asked by many is "What is an internship?". An internship, is the act of working at a business for not money but experience

That is the main point of this project, I would like to get people to start experiencing their jobs head-on.

I am hoping to at some point get a sign up going for getting into internships from here at LEAD and possibly highschools starting FRESHMAN year.(preferably a website)
